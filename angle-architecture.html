About Me

I am a starting UX/UI designer, aspiring to grow my expertise in designing digital products.

I have shifted my career from front-end developer to the UX/UI design because I enjoy the process of coming up with ideas, learning about the user needs, applying strategies, to seeing the final designs. As a first step, I have taken the Google UX Design Certificate, that helped me acquire fundamental knowledge about the UX process. I have also completed along three projects that are presented in this portfolio.

I am looking forward for opportunities to apply my creativity, attention to details, curiosity to learn, and test innovative ideas on projects that are inspiring, useful, and with a positive impact, all while continually learning and growing as a designer and as a person.

Angle-Architecture

The website is for a small architecture bureau that specifies on public buildings projects and wich is growing the team and business and wants to extend the client base from local to international.

The main role of the website was to connect the business with new clients, locals as well as international, by showcasing the projects the agency has done in the past, and present relevant information about their work approach, as well as to offer contact details. Also, the website matches the brand identity and communicates clearly their values and mission.

Takeaways

As the first full project that I worked on, I learned a lot about the process of design itself. I learned about each stage of design as it went on: strategy, competitors analysis, content structuring, wireframes, mood-board, creating prototypes, design systems, development part.

I also gain experience of working with grids, responsive layout, creating a design system, as well as defining a strategy, based on analysis, and creating the design solution around it.
